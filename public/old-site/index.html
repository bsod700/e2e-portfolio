About

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Guy Tagger, a Front-End Developer and UI/UX Designer with a knack for crafting beautiful and functional websites and apps.I build responsive, mobile-friendly sites using Angular, HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, and TypeScript, always following best practices for clean and efficient code.When it comes to design, I use Figma, Adobe XD, Illustrator, and Photoshop to create wireframes, mockups, interactive prototypes, animations, logos, slides, and other branding materials, ensuring top-notch user experiences.
